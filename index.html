About Me

Hello, my name is Jules Danzig. I am a Video Editor and Digital Music Composer. My primary focus in videos falls towards video game skits with a value in storytelling, along with some comedic undertones. My music falls towards the Electronic/EDM genres with various amounts of unconventional sounds. My overall hope for the future is to find a career in the areas of Video Game Design and Video Game music production.

What do I do?

Video Editing

Various length short films and comedic skits with post effects and music sync., primarily using Davinci Resolve.

Digital Music Production / Composition

Rhythms and melodies composed with different unique sounds, all directed toward certain genres including EDM, and electronic. Primarily made using Ableton Live

Graphic Design

Logos and thumbnails put together in order to attract an audience and deliver a presentation of character. Done primarily in Photoshop and Illustrator.
